2. **Team members:** Read `BENTO_GRID_QUICK_REFERENCE.md` (5 min each)
3. **Project manager:** Read `BENTO_GRID_WORKLOAD_ALLOCATION.md` (20 min)
4. **Workstream owners:** Read your section of `BENTO_GRID_WORKSTREAM_DETAILS.md` (15 min)
5. **All:** Discuss `BENTO_GRID_RISK_MITIGATION.md` overview (15 min)

**Total kickoff time:** 1 hour

### For Weekly Planning
1. Check `BENTO_GRID_WORKLOAD_ALLOCATION.md` Section 4 (This week's schedule)
2. Review your workstream's weekly breakdown
3. Check risks in `BENTO_GRID_RISK_MITIGATION.md`
4. Confirm approval gates this week

### For Issue Resolution
1. Check `BENTO_GRID_RISK_MITIGATION.md` (search for your issue)
2. Find mitigation strategy and contingency plan
3. Follow escalation procedure if needed

### For Decision Making
1. CEO checks relevant approval gate in `BENTO_GRID_QUICK_REFERENCE.md`
2. Review success criteria in `BENTO_GRID_WORKLOAD_ALLOCATION.md` Section 9
3. Make decision with full context
