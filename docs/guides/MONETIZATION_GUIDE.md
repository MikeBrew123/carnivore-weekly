## Ad Placements

### 1. **Premium Sponsor Banner** (Top of page)
- **Location**: Right after header, before content
- **Style**: Dark leather background, prominent CTA button
- **Best for**: Main sponsor, highest visibility
- **Example**: ButcherBox, Crowd Cow, US Wellness Meats

### 2. **Inline Ad #1** (Mid-content)
- **Location**: Between Top Videos and Key Insights
- **Style**: Subtle tan background, integrated look
- **Best for**: Supplements, electrolytes
- **Example**: LMNT, Redmond Real Salt, Perfect Keto

### 3. **Inline Ad #2** (Lower content)
- **Location**: Between Community Voice and Recommended Watching
- **Style**: Same subtle style as Inline Ad #1
- **Best for**: Products, cookware, books
- **Example**: Lodge Cast Iron, Carnivore MD Book, Traeger Grills

## How to Implement

### Update the Template
Edit `/templates/index_template.html` and replace the example ads with real affiliate links:

```html
<!-- Premium Sponsor Banner -->
<div class="sponsor-banner">
    <h3>Premium Grass-Fed Beef Delivered to Your Door</h3>
    <p>Get 100% grass-fed beef from ButcherBox...</p>
    <a href="YOUR_AFFILIATE_LINK" class="sponsor-cta">Get $20 Off</a>
</div>
```

## Best Practices

1. **Keep it relevant** - Only promote products you'd use yourself
2. **Disclose affiliates** - Be transparent (already have disclaimer)
3. **Test and rotate** - Track which ads perform best
4. **Don't oversaturate** - 3-4 ads total is plenty
5. **Match the vibe** - Only carnivore-friendly, quality products
