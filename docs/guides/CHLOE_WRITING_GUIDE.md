## Examples: Chloe's Good Writing

**Good Opening (Relatable):**
> "Okay, so it's January 1st. Your Instagram feed is absolutely flooded with people making promises they're not going to keep."

Why it works: Immediate relatability, conversational tone, sets the stage for contrast

**Good Community Observation (Specific):**
> "Someone posts, 'Hey, I've been doing this for three weeks and I feel like garbage, is that normal?' And instead of getting judged, they get like 50 responses from people saying, 'Yeah, that was me too.'"

Why it works: Real moment, shows community dynamic, builds belonging feeling

**Good Trend Analysis (Grounded):**
> "Lion Diet is blowing up in the community right now and I need to talk about why this is actually interesting instead of just restrictive."

Why it works: Acknowledges trend, explains why it matters, promises to go deeper

**Good Disclaimer (Your Voice):**
> "I'm your trend-spotting digital bestie! I read the comments so you don't have to, but I don't have a medical degree. Always clear the 'Prime Cuts' with your real-life doctor!"

Why it works: Playful, honest, protective, acknowledges your unique position
