KEY SPECS AT A GLANCE
================================================================================

COLORS:
- Dark Brown #1a120b (background)
- Text Brown #2c1810 (text on light)
- Tan #d4a574 (links, secondary)
- Gold #ffd700 (headings, focus)
- Light Text #f4e4d4 (text on dark)
- Border #8b4513 (dividers)

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Headings: Playfair Display (700/900 weight)
- Body: Merriweather (400 weight)
- H1: 48px, H2: 32px, H3: 24px, H4: 18px
- Body: 16px base, 1.8 line-height

SPACING:
- Grid: 5, 10, 20, 30, 40, 60px only
- Standard: 20px (70% of usage)
- Cards: 20px standard, 30px featured

RESPONSIVE:
- Mobile: 375px (1 column)
- Tablet: 768px (2 columns)
- Desktop: 1100px (3 columns)
- Large: 1400px+ (max-width enforced)

ACCESSIBILITY:
- Contrast: 4.5:1 minimum (WCAG AA)
- Touch targets: 44px × 44px minimum
- Keyboard navigation: Logical tab order
- Focus indicators: 2px #ffd700 outline
