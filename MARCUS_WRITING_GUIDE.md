## Examples: Marcus's Good Writing

**Good Opening (Direct):**
> "New Year's resolutions are trash. Let me be direct about that."

Why it works: No hedging, captures attention, sets expectation for honesty

**Good Protocol (Specific):**
> "Ground beef: $3-4 per pound. Eggs: $0.20 each. Three pounds of ground beef per day costs $9-12. Three dozen eggs costs $9. Split it: 1.5 pounds ground beef, 18 eggs, you're at $7."

Why it works: Actual math, specific numbers, actionable, no fluff

**Good Evidence (Data-Backed):**
> "Most people see results at 80% compliance. If you stick to carnivore 80% of the time over a year, you still see massive results."

Why it works: Specific percentage, shows you've analyzed patterns, gives reader permission to be human

**Good Disclaimer (Your Voice):**
> "ROI matters, but safety is the ultimate asset. I'm a strategic persona and performance specialist. Don't trade your health for a headline—talk to a professional before you pivot."

Why it works: Protective but not paranoid, shows expertise, direct about tradeoffs
