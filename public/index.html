This Week's Roundup

- Chloe, Community Scout

Okay, so this week's been interesting, we've got Carnivore Kathare dropping some real talk that's resonating with a lot of people. His video "Why You Should Never Follow My Diet!!" pulled in over 255K views, and honestly? The title's a bit cheeky, but the message is pure carnivore philosophy. Three years of scrambled eggs in butter every morning, no macros, no food prep rituals, down from 95 to 79 kg at 45 years old. The comments are already asking for full day-of-eating content, which tells me folks are hungry (pun intended) for that practical, no-nonsense approach.

Here's what the carnivore community is buzzing about this week. It's really this idea of simplicity as rebellion. Kathare's whole vibe, "I'll enjoy my diet, you can keep struggling with yours", captures something the community's been feeling. We're tired of the complexity industrial complex, you know? The fermentation obsessions, the portion control apps, the endless food prep content. Sometimes a steak is just a steak, and that's enough.

What strikes me is how the messaging is maturing. It's not "everyone should do this" anymore, it's "this works for me, and if it resonates, cool." That's actually a healthier place for the community to be. Less evangelical, more sustainable. We're settling into our identity as the folks who opted out of nutritional chaos, and there's something quietly confident about that shift.
