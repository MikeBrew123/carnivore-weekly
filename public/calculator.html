Stop guessing. Get your actual macros in 2 minutes.

Most people starting carnivore guess at their macros. Then they wonder why they're exhausted, losing muscle, or not seeing results. One thing that helps immediately: knowing your actual protein target. Most people eat way too little—or way too much. If you want to skip the guessing and get your exact macros in about 2 minutes, we built a free calculator that personalizes your targets based on your body and your goal (fat loss, muscle gain, or maintenance).

It's free. No email required. Just enter your stats, pick your goal, and get your numbers. You can print your results or upgrade to the $9.99 report if you want meal plans and a doctor's consultation script—but the free version gives you everything you actually need to start.
