Why This Exists

Every single day, I found myself doing the same thing: searching YouTube for the latest carnivore content. Scrolling through Dr. Ken Berry's channel. Checking what Shawn Baker posted. Looking for Kelly Hogan's updates. Reading through hundreds of comments to see what people were actually experiencing.

I wanted to know what the influencers were saying. I wanted to know what real people thought about what they were saying. I wanted to see if there were studies backing up the claims. I wanted success stories, controversies, debates-all of it.

And I thought: If I'm spending an hour a day searching for this stuff, there's no way I'm the only one.

So I built this. A weekly digest that puts everything I was looking for in one place. Fresh content from the top creators. Community sentiment from the comments. Scientific citations when available. The good, the bad, and the controversial-all curated and organized.

If I want to see it, I figured you probably do too.

What You Get

  • Top Videos from Leading Creators - I track the biggest names in carnivore and pull their most-viewed, most-discussed content from the past week.
  • Community Voice - Real comments, real sentiment, real success stories. Not just what influencers say, but what the community thinks.
  • Trending Topics - What's being debated this week? What's gaining traction? What questions keep coming up?
  • Evidence-Based Answers - Common questions answered with scientific citations when available. No BS, just facts.
  • All in One Place - No more tab overload. No more searching. Just one weekly roundup with everything you need.

Who I Am

I'm a 20-year firefighter and structure protection specialist. My job demands peak physical and mental performance. I can't afford brain fog, energy crashes, or inflammation slowing me down when lives are on the line.

I'm also a CrossFitter. I need fuel that builds strength, supports recovery, and keeps me operating at full capacity. The carnivore diet does that for me in ways nothing else has.

But I'm not a doctor. I'm not a scientist. I'm not selling supplements or meal plans. I'm just a guy who wanted a better way to stay informed about this lifestyle without spending hours hunting for information.

This isn't a business. It's a resource I built for myself and decided to share. That's it.

How It Works

Every week, I collect data from the top carnivore YouTube channels. I analyze view counts, engagement, comments, and trending topics. I look for patterns, controversies, success stories, and common questions.

Then I package it all into one clean, organized page. You get the highlights without the noise. The insights without the overwhelm. The community pulse without scrolling through thousands of comments.

It updates every Monday. Bookmark it. Make it part of your routine. Stay informed without the time sink.

My Promise

I'm not here to sell you anything beyond affiliate links to products I'd recommend anyway (quality cookware, electrolytes, grass-fed meat delivery). If you buy through them, great-it keeps this site running. If not, no hard feelings.

I'm not pushing an agenda. I'm not cherry-picking data to fit a narrative. If there's controversy in the community, you'll see it. If studies contradict claims, I'll cite them. If someone has a different take, it'll be included.

This is carnivore content, unfiltered and uncurated for bias-just organized for convenience.
