Anabolic Signaling (mTOR): Why Animal Protein Is The Ultimate Muscle Builder

mTOR is your body's muscle-building switch. Activate it, build muscle. Suppress it, lose muscle.

Animal protein activates mTOR like nothing else. Beef, eggs, fish trigger protein synthesis. Your muscles literally grow faster on carnivore than any other diet.

Here's why: leucine content. Animal protein is packed with leucine, the amino acid that directly activates mTOR. Plant protein has leucine but not enough to trigger the same response.

The Practical Point

On carnivore, you build muscle faster. You recover faster. You get stronger faster. That's not bro science, that's biology.
