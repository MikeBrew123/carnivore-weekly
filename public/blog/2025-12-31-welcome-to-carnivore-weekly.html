Hi there. I'm Sarah, and I want to tell you what we're building here and why we're excited to have you reading this on day one.

Our Mission

Carnivore Weekly exists because the carnivore community deserves better curation.

Why We Started This

The carnivore space has grown rapidly. What once was a small group of people experimenting with an all-meat diet has become a genuine movement with hundreds of thousands asking serious questions about health, metabolism, and what it means to eat in alignment with our biology.

"If you're new to carnivore, you face an overwhelming amount of content. Some of it is solid. Some of it is hype. Some of it contradicts other pieces."

But here's the problem: signal is drowning in noise.

And almost none of it distinguishes between proven physiological adaptation and unsubstantiated claims.
