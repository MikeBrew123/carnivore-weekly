The ADHD Connection: How Stable Blood Sugar and Ketones Fix Focus

ADHD isn't always neurological. Sometimes it's metabolic.

When your blood sugar swings wildly (carbs spike, insulin crashes), your dopamine and cortisol follow the same pattern. Up. Down. Scattered. Foggy. Can't focus.

Carnivore stabilizes blood sugar completely. No spikes. No crashes. Constant, steady energy source from fat and ketones.

Your brain runs better on ketones than glucose anyway. Ketones are a cleaner fuel. More stable energy production. Less oxidative stress.

What People Report

Better focus. Sustained attention. No mid-afternoon crash. Ability to work for 4-6 hours without breaking concentration.

Some people find they no longer "need" their ADHD meds. Some reduce their dose. Some stay on meds but feel clearer with carnivore as the foundation.

The Mechanism

Blood sugar stability = dopamine stability = focus and motivation.

Add in the reduced inflammation from cutting processed food, and your brain literally functions better.

Not a cure. But a massive improvement in baseline function.
