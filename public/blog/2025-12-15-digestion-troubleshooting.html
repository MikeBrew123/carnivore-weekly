Digestion Issues on Carnivore: Troubleshooting

Carnivore usually improves digestion. But sometimes things go sideways. Here's how to fix it.

Loose Stool (Week 1-2)

Normal? Yes. Your microbiome is shifting. Bile production is changing.

Fix: Wait 2-3 weeks. Usually resolves. If persistent: reduce fat slightly, add more salt, stay hydrated.

Constipation (Week 2+)

Cause: Usually undereating fat or not drinking enough water.

Fix: Add more fat to meals (butter, fatty cuts). Increase water intake. If still stuck: magnesium supplement can help (200-400mg).

Bloating/Gas

Cause: Often overeating at once. Your stomach is smaller now.

Fix: Eat smaller meals more frequently. Chew thoroughly. Slow down eating pace.

Reflux/Heartburn

Cause: Usually too much fat too fast, or not enough salt/hydration.

Fix: Eat less at once. Salt your food. Drink water. Usually improves in weeks 3-4.

When to Worry

Persistent pain, blood in stool, or severe symptoms past week 3 → See a doctor. Might not be carnivore-compatible for you.

Bottom Line

Most digestion issues resolve by week 4. Patience + adjustments usually fix it.
