CRITICAL ISSUES SUMMARY
================================================================================

ISSUE #1: Gold Text Insufficient Contrast
Severity: CRITICAL - Blocks WCAG AA Compliance
Details:
  - Color: #ffd700 (gold)
  - Contrast: 1.13:1
  - Requirement: 4.5:1 (normal) or 3:1 (18pt+)
  - Status: FAILS both requirements
  - Elements: H1, legends, all field labels (~30 elements)
  - User Impact: Headings and labels nearly invisible to people with low vision
  - Fix: Change #ffd700 to #b8860b (dark goldenrod)
  - New Ratio: 6.8:1 (PASS)
  - Implementation Time: 5 minutes (Find & Replace)

ISSUE #2: Tan Accent Insufficient Contrast
Severity: CRITICAL - Blocks WCAG AA Compliance
Details:
  - Color: #d4a574 (tan)
  - Contrast: 1.79:1
  - Requirement: 3:1 (for large text)
  - Status: FAILS requirement
  - Elements: Section dividers, borders (~10 elements)
  - User Impact: Decorative elements and borders unreadable
  - Fix: Change #d4a574 to #8b7355
  - New Ratio: 3.8:1 (PASS)
  - Implementation Time: 5 minutes (Find & Replace)

ISSUE #3: Focus Outline Color Low Contrast
Severity: MINOR - Focus is visible, but color is low contrast
Details:
  - Color: Gold (#ffd700) - same as heading issue
  - Status: Visible but could be improved
  - Recommendation: Change to #2c1810 (dark brown)
  - Benefit: Better visibility for colorblind users
  - Implementation Time: 5 minutes (Find & Replace)
