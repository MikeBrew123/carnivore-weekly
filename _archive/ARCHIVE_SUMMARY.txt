================================================================================
                         HOW TO RECOVER
================================================================================

If you find something is broken and need a file:

1. Check _archive/RESTORATION_GUIDE.md for detailed recovery instructions
2. Determine which file you need from the archive
3. Copy from _archive/ to project root:

   cp _archive/reports/ACTIVATION_SUMMARY.txt .
   cp _archive/deployment-scripts/deploy-migration.js .
   cp _archive/test-scripts/test-quick.mjs .

4. Or restore entire category:

   cp _archive/test-scripts/* .

5. Or restore everything:

   cp -r _archive/* .

⚠️  IMPORTANT: Before using deployment scripts, check for hardcoded credentials
    and sanitize before use.
