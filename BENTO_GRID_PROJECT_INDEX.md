## Escalation & Issue Resolution

### When to Escalate

**Immediate Escalation (Same Day):**
- Design not approved by Fri Jan 10
- Critical feature bug found
- Team member unavailable
- Content approval blocked
- Go/no-go decision needed

**Escalation Process:**
1. Mention in standup (9 AM)
2. Jordan notifies CEO (10 AM)
3. CEO decides (same day): Continue with mitigation, escalate for team decision, or change course
4. If major impact: Team meeting next morning

### Who Decides What

**Design Direction:** CEO + Casey
**Development Approach:** Jordan
**Content Approval:** Sarah + Editor-in-Chief
**Timeline & Scope:** CEO
**Risk Management:** CEO (with Jordan input)
