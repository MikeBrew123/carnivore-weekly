This Week's Roundup

- Chloe, Community Scout

Okay so this week was kind of all over the place in carnivore YouTube land. THE STORY absolutely dominated with over 4 million views, I'm guessing another viral transformation or celebrity carnivore moment, because those numbers don't happen quietly. Core Instinct and iRick Wiggins also had solid weeks pulling in the curious crowds. What I'm noticing is this split between the big splashy viral content and the steady educational stuff from folks like Dr. Chaffee and Dr. Berry, who are just quietly doing their thing in the mid-six-figures. Honestly? Both serve a purpose.

The practical corner of carnivore YouTube is thriving too, Monique, Meal Prep on Fleek, and Steak and Butter Gal are all putting up real numbers, which tells me people aren't just looking for why anymore, they want the how. Like, "great, I'm convinced, now what do I actually cook on a Tuesday." Speaking of keeping it simple, Marcus wrote this whole thing about ground beef and eggs being the underrated baseline and honestly it tracks with the vibe I'm seeing, people want sustainable, not fancy.

What's got me thinking though: with Coach Carnivore Cam growing and all these meal prep channels gaining traction, are we finally past the "is this safe?" phase and into the "okay but how do I actually live like this" era? Because if so, that's kind of a big deal. What's been your go-to easy meal this week?

🔥🔥 Trending Steak and Butter Gal

This Is The New Butter (And You’ll Be FORCED To Eat It!)

Okay, so the community is rightfully fired up about this one. What's striking isn't just the butter itself, it's the pattern people are noticing: ingredients changing quietly, labeling getting fuzzier, and regulatory bodies that seem more interested in protecting corporate interests than consumers. That's hitting a nerve because carnivores are already hyperaware of what's in their food, and when your diet is built on whole foods, additives feel like a betrayal. The farm-to-table comments aren't nostalgia, they're a wake-up call that local sourcing might actually become necessary again, not trendy. This is exactly why so many people in the community are reconnecting with local farmers and asking harder questions about their protein sources.

- Chloe

▶
🔥🔥 Trending Steak and Butter Gal

I'm Eating This ENTIRE Pizza Everyday and Losing Weight

The comments here are telling the real story. Someone reversed heart disease and lost 60 pounds. Another person ditched daily Tylenol for headaches. These aren't small wins, and they're not accidents. What Steak and Butter Gal is showing is that eating carnivore doesn't mean deprivation. it means swapping the foods that were hurting you for versions that actually satisfy. The cottage cheese crust idea is genius because it solves the biggest psychological barrier people face when they start. You don't have to white-knuckle your way through cravings or convince yourself steamed chicken is a treat. You get actual pizza, the texture you're craving, the satisfaction you remember. That's why this works where most diets fail, and why the weight loss becomes almost inevitable when the food stops fighting against you.

- Sarah
